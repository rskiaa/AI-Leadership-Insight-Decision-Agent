Northstar Dynamics Operational Update - January 2026

Current Status
The operations team completed migration of 72 percent of customers to the unified platform. Remaining migrations are concentrated in custom enterprise environments and older regional deployments.

Delivery and Support
Average implementation duration improved from 14 weeks to 11 weeks for standard deployments.
Customer support satisfaction rose from 4.1 to 4.5 out of 5 after expanding self-service documentation and introducing guided triage.
The professional services team is operating near full capacity, especially for customers requesting AI workflow configuration.

People and Resourcing
Hiring progress remains mixed. Product and customer success roles are tracking to plan, while hiring for senior AI engineers and solutions architects is behind target.
Leadership approved additional contractor support for implementation and documentation work through the first half of 2026.

Process Improvements
A new cross-functional weekly review now tracks launch readiness, enterprise escalations, onboarding delays, and product defect trends.
The top operational issue remains handoff quality between sales and implementation teams, particularly when custom integrations are involved.

Immediate Priorities
Reduce implementation backlog for high-value enterprise customers.
Improve sales-to-delivery handoff discipline.
Stabilize staffing for AI-related deployment work.
